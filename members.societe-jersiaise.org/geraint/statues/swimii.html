Swimmers II

 

 

A sculpture by Philip Jackson, also responsible for the Liberation sculpture, which is situated in Les Jardins de la Mer, at the centre of a water/hedge maze. The maze was designed by Adrian Fisher.

 

 

Compare the dolphins with the Westaway monument, the Dolphin in the Fish Market, and the dolphins on the States Building.

 

 

 

It was opened to the public on 19 July 1997 as part of Les Jardins de la Mer. However it is another in the tradition of 'moving' statues, having been set up in the Esplanade car park for a time while money was raised to acquire the piece.
