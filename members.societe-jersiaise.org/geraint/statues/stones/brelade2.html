St. Brelade - St Brélade

 


To the glory of God

 

Meeting at the cross roads

The St Brelade Millennium cross, marking 2,000 years of Christianity, was dedicated last Saturday by the Rector, the Rev Noel Carter, at a ceremony attended by about 60 people, including the Constable, Max de la Haye. The cross stands at the junction of Pont du Val and Route des Quennevais and a time capsule is to be buried below it.

JEP 7/2/2000
