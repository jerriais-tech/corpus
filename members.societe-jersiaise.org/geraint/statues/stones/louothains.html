Rolling stone appears in St. Lawrence

The St. Lawrence Millennium standing stone was put into position on Sunday morning at the Vicart picnic area in waterworks valley. The second standing stone to be raised, it was put into position by a team from the Jersey Field Squadron. Those present included St. Lawrence Constable Iris Le Feuvre and Société Jersiaise president Philip Le Brocq. The next stone is due to be raised next Saturday in St. Brelade.

JEP 6/9/1999
